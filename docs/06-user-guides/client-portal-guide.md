## Getting Started

### First-Time Access

You'll receive an email invitation from your consulting firm with:
- Your portal URL (e.g., `https://firmname.consultantpro.com/portal`)
- Instructions to create your password

**To set up your account:**

1. Click the link in your email invitation
2. Create a secure password (12+ characters)
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You're logged in and ready to go!

### Logging In

**Portal URL:** Provided by your consulting firm  
**Example:** `https://firmname.consultantpro.com/portal`

1. Navigate to your portal URL
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click **Log In**

**Forgot Password?**
1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Follow instructions to create new password

## Viewing Projects

### Projects Dashboard

See all your projects at a glance:

1. Click **Projects** in main menu
2. View:
   - **Project Name**
   - **Status** (Active, On Hold, Completed)
   - **Progress** (percentage complete)
   - **Next Milestone**

### Project Details

Click on a project to see:

#### Overview Tab
- **Description** - What the project is about
- **Timeline** - Start and end dates
- **Project Manager** - Your main contact
- **Status** - Current state

#### Tasks Tab
- **Task List** - All project tasks
- **Status** - To Do, In Progress, Review, Done
- **Assigned To** - Who's working on it
- **Due Date** - When it's due

**Note:** You can view tasks but cannot edit them. Contact your project manager if you have questions.

#### Timeline Tab
- **Milestones** - Major deliverables
- **Progress** - Overall completion percentage
- **Key Dates** - Important deadlines

#### Files Tab
- **Project Documents** - Files related to this project
- Download deliverables
- View shared presentations or reports

## Communication

### Messaging System

Communicate securely with your consulting team:

#### Sending a Message

1. Click **Messages** in main menu
2. Click **New Message**
3. Select:
   - **Recipient** (your project manager or team)
   - **Subject**
4. Type your message
5. **Attach files** (optional)
6. Click **Send**

#### Reading Messages

1. Click **Messages** in main menu
2. Click on message to read
3. Click **Reply** to respond

**Message Categories:**
- **Inbox** - New messages
- **Sent** - Messages you sent
- **Archive** - Old messages

#### Message Notifications

You'll receive email notifications when:
- You receive a new message
- Someone replies to your message
- Project updates are shared

**To manage notifications:**
1. Click **Profile** → **Notification Settings**
2. Toggle notifications on/off
3. Click **Save**

## Invoices & Payments

### Viewing Invoices

See all your invoices:

1. Click **Invoices** in main menu
2. View invoice list:
   - **Invoice Number**
   - **Date**
   - **Amount**
   - **Status** (Pending, Paid, Overdue)

#### Invoice Details

Click on an invoice to see:
- **Line Items** - Services provided
- **Amounts** - Subtotal, tax, total
- **Payment Terms** - Due date
- **Payment Status** - Paid or pending

#### Downloading Invoices

1. Open invoice
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. Save to your computer

**Use for:**
- Your accounting records
- Expense reimbursement
- Tax documentation

### Paying Invoices Online

**If online payment is enabled:**

1. Open unpaid invoice
2. Click **Pay Now**
3. Choose payment method:
   - **Credit Card**
   - **Bank Account (ACH)**
4. Enter payment details
5. Review amount
6. Click **Submit Payment**
7. You'll receive confirmation email

**Payment Processing:**
- Credit card: Instant
- Bank account (ACH): 3-5 business days

### Payment Methods

#### Adding a Payment Method

1. Click **Profile** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Choose type:
   - **Credit/Debit Card**
   - **Bank Account**
4. Enter details securely
5. Click **Save**

**Security:** All payment information is encrypted and secure. Your consulting firm never sees your full card number or bank account details.

#### Setting Up Autopay

Enable automatic payment of invoices:

1. Click **Profile** → **Billing**
2. Click **Enable Autopay**
3. Select:
   - **Payment Method** (credit card or bank)
   - **When to Charge** (on due date or X days before)
4. Review terms
5. Click **Enable**

**How Autopay Works:**
- Invoices are charged automatically when due
- You receive confirmation email after each charge
- You can still view invoice details
- You can disable autopay anytime

**Benefits:**
- Never miss a payment
- No manual action needed
- Build good payment history

#### Viewing Payment History

1. Click **Invoices** → **Payment History**
2. See all payments:
   - **Date**
   - **Amount**
   - **Payment Method**
   - **Invoice(s) Paid**

## Documents

### Document Library

Access all shared documents:

1. Click **Documents** in main menu
2. Browse folders:
   - **Contracts** - Agreements and terms
   - **Deliverables** - Project outputs
   - **Reports** - Analysis and findings
   - **Presentations** - Slide decks
   - **Other** - Miscellaneous files

### Viewing Documents

1. Navigate to folder
2. Click on document name
3. Document opens in browser (if supported) or downloads

### Downloading Documents

**Single Document:**
1. Click on document
2. Click **Download** button
3. Save to your computer

**Multiple Documents:**
1. Select documents (checkboxes)
2. Click **Download Selected**
3. Downloads as ZIP file

### Document Versions

Some documents have multiple versions:

1. Open document
2. Click **Versions** tab
3. See all versions:
   - **Version Number**
   - **Upload Date**
   - **Uploaded By**
   - **Notes** (what changed)
4. Click **Download** for specific version

**Latest version is always shown by default.**

### Uploading Documents

**If enabled by your consulting firm:**

1. Navigate to appropriate folder
2. Click **Upload**
3. Select file(s) from your computer
4. Add description (optional)
5. Click **Upload**

**Common uses:**
- Provide requested documents (contracts, data, etc.)
- Submit deliverables (if collaborative project)
- Share feedback or redlines

## Profile Settings

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click **Profile** in menu
2. Update:
   - **Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Company** (your organization)
   - **Title** (your role)
3. Click **Save**

### Changing Password

1. Click **Profile** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter:
   - **Current Password**
   - **New Password**
   - **Confirm New Password**
4. Click **Update Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 12 characters
- Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers
- At least one special character

### Notification Preferences

Control what emails you receive:

1. Click **Profile** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle on/off:
   - **New Messages** - Chat/message notifications
   - **Project Updates** - Project progress updates
   - **Invoice Reminders** - Payment due reminders
   - **Document Shares** - New document notifications
3. Click **Save**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Can I access the portal from my mobile device?**  
A: Yes! The portal is mobile-responsive and works on phones and tablets.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes. ConsultantPro uses bank-level encryption. Your consulting firm follows strict privacy policies.

**Q: Can I share my portal access with colleagues?**  
A: No. Each person needs their own account. Contact your consulting firm to add additional portal users.

### Projects

**Q: Why can't I edit tasks?**  
A: Tasks are managed by your consulting team. If you need changes, message your project manager.

**Q: How do I know when a milestone is complete?**  
A: Completed milestones are marked with a ✓ checkmark. You'll also receive a notification.

**Q: Can I see who's working on my project?**  
A: Yes. Go to project details and see the **Team** section.

### Invoices & Payments

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Typically credit card and bank account (ACH). Check with your consulting firm for specifics.

**Q: When will my payment be processed?**  
A: Credit cards are instant. Bank transfers take 3-5 business days.

**Q: Can I pay by check or wire transfer?**  
A: Yes, but these must be arranged directly with your consulting firm. The portal only supports credit card and ACH.

**Q: What if my payment fails?**  
A: You'll receive an email notification. Update your payment method and retry, or contact your consulting firm.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refunds are handled by your consulting firm. Contact them directly to discuss.

### Documents

**Q: How long are documents stored?**  
A: Depends on document type. Important documents (contracts, invoices) are retained for 7+ years. Ask your consulting firm about specific retention policies.

**Q: Can I delete documents?**  
A: No. Only your consulting firm can delete documents. This protects important records.

**Q: What file types are supported?**  
A: Most common types: PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images, etc. Maximum file size: 100 MB.
